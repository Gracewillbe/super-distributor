## Content Calendar

**Claude Code skill for Super Distributor**

Generate a 30/60/90-day content calendar to maintain consistent, valuable social presence without burning out.

---

## What It Does

Creates a complete content calendar tailored to your product and audience.

**You provide:**
- Product description and target audience
- Main goal (awareness, users, engagement)
- Platform focus (Twitter, LinkedIn, Reddit, or multi-platform)
- Posting frequency

**You get:**
- Day-by-day content topics
- Format recommendations (posts, threads, visuals)
- Platform-specific adaptations
- Build-in-public story arcs
- Copyable spreadsheet format

---

## Calendar Options

### 30 Days
One month of planned content

### 60 Days (Recommended)
Two months - ideal for launch prep

### 90 Days
Full quarter - for long-term planning

---

## Content Pillars

Your calendar will mix these themes:

**Build in Public**
- Progress updates
- Challenges and solutions
- Metrics and milestones

**Value & Education**
- Tips and how-tos
- Lessons learned
- Frameworks

**Community & Conversation**
- Questions
- Opinions
- User stories

**Product & Features**
- Announcements
- Use cases
- Behind-the-scenes

---

## Format Mix

**60%** Short posts (1-3 sentences)
**25%** Threads (5-10 tweets)
**10%** Visual posts (screenshots, diagrams)
**5%** Long-form (blog links, tutorials)

---

## Story Arcs

Content follows narrative progression:

**Pre-Launch (Weeks 1-4)**
- Introduce problem
- Share build progress
- Build anticipation

**Launch (Weeks 5-6)**
- Announce
- Share results
- Iterate publicly

**Post-Launch (Weeks 7+)**
- User stories
- Lessons learned
- Build community

---

## How to Use

### In Claude Code

```
Use the content-calendar skill
```

The skill will guide you through:
1. Choose calendar length (30/60/90 days)
2. Define product and audience
3. Set posting frequency
4. Select content pillars
5. Generate day-by-day calendar

## Prerequisites

**You should have:**
- ✅ Product messaging (use `messaging-generator`)

- ✅ Platform account set up

## Platform Recommendations

### Twitter/X
- Best for: Builders, developers, product people
- Frequency: 1-2x daily
- Format: Short posts + threads

### LinkedIn
- Best for: B2B, professional services
- Frequency: 2-3x weekly
- Format: Longer posts (1000+ chars)

### Reddit
- Best for: Niche communities, early feedback
- Frequency: 1-2x weekly
- Format: Value-first, not promotional

---

## Execution Tips

### Before Starting
- [ ] Batch create 2-3 weeks of content
- [ ] Set up scheduling tool
- [ ] Block 30 min daily for engagement

### While Posting
- [ ] Engage with replies (don't just post and ghost)
- [ ] Share others' content too
- [ ] Reply within first hour

### After 30 Days
- [ ] Review what performed well
- [ ] Double down on winners
- [ ] Update calendar based on data

---

## Avoiding Burnout

**The 80/20 Rule**
- 80% results from 20% of posts
- Quality > quantity

**Batch Content**
- Write 5-10 posts in one sitting
- Schedule in advance

**Repurpose**
- Threads → blog posts
- Blog posts → threads
- Comments → new posts

**Take Breaks**
- It's okay to skip days
- Consistency > perfection
