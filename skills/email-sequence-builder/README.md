## Email Sequence Builder

**Claude Code skill for Super Distributor**

Generate complete email sequences to turn visitors into active, engaged users.

---

## What It Does

Creates ready-to-use email sequences for every stage of the user journey.

**You provide:**
- Product information
- Sequence type (welcome, onboarding, nurture, re-engagement)
- Tone and timing preferences

**You get:**
- Complete email sequences with subject lines and body copy
- Personalization merge tags
- Recommended timing and cadence
- Setup instructions for email tools

---

## Email Sequences Available

### 1. Welcome Series (5 emails over 7 days)
For new waitlist signups or subscribers
- Email 1: Welcome + immediate value
- Email 2: Core value explanation
- Email 3: Quick win / use case
- Email 4: Community + resources
- Email 5: First ask

### 2. Onboarding (4 emails over 10 days)
For users who just signed up or started using
- Email 1: Welcome + first action
- Email 2: Feature spotlight
- Email 3: Success story / use case
- Email 4: Milestone check-in

### 3. Nurture (Ongoing, every 1-2 weeks)
For keeping active users engaged
- Product updates
- Tips & tricks
- Community highlights
- Feedback requests

### 4. Re-engagement (3 emails over 7 days)
For inactive users
- Email 1: We miss you
- Email 2: What you're missing
- Email 3: Last call

## Best Practices

### Subject Lines
✅ 5-7 words, specific
✅ Personal, not salesy
❌ No "Re:" tricks or ALL CAPS

### Body Copy
✅ One idea per email
✅ Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
✅ Clear single CTA
❌ No walls of text

### Timing
✅ Work hours (9 AM - 5 PM)
✅ Space emails 2+ days apart
❌ Don't email daily

### Deliverability
✅ Real email address (not noreply@)
✅ Domain authentication (SPF, DKIM)
✅ Easy unsubscribe
❌ No purchased lists

## Success Metrics

**Good benchmarks:**
- Open rate: 20-30%
- Click rate: 3-5%
- Reply rate: 1-2%
- Unsubscribe: <0.5%

**If metrics are low:**
- Low opens → Test subject lines
- Low clicks → Clarify CTA
- High unsubscribes → Reduce frequency

---

## Common Mistakes

❌ **Too many emails too fast** - Space them out
❌ **Multiple CTAs per email** - Pick one action
❌ **Using noreply@ address** - Kills engagement
❌ **Not testing first** - Always send test emails
❌ **Forgetting to unsubscribe inactive users** - Hurts deliverability
