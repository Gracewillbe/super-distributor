# Messaging Generator

**Claude Code skill for Super Distributor**

Turn your positioning into clear, compelling messaging that sticks.

---

## What It Does

This skill guides you through the **Messaging Framework** from Chapter 04 - Messaging.

**You provide:**
- Your positioning inputs (problem, solution, who it's for, what makes you different)

**You get:**
- 3 one-liner variations (different proven formats)
- Value proposition (30-second pitch)
- 3-5 core message pillars
- Adapted messaging for different platforms (Twitter, landing page, Product Hunt)

---

## How to Use

### In Claude Code

```
Use the messaging-generator skill
```

The skill will ask you 6 questions about your product, then generate your complete messaging hierarchy.

---

## What You'll Get

### 1. Three One-Liner Variations

Using proven formats:
- **Format A:** [What] for [Who]
- **Format B:** [What] without [Pain]
- **Format C:** The [Category] that [Unique Approach]

Pick the one that feels most natural to say out loud.

---

### 2. Value Proposition (30-second version)

Three sentences:
1. Your one-liner
2. The problem you solve
3. How you solve it differently

---

### 3. Core Message Pillars (3-5 pillars)

The architecture of your messaging:
- **Problem** - Why this exists
- **Solution** - What it does
- **Proof** - Why trust you
- **Access** - What's the catch
- **Vision** - What's the bigger idea (optional)

---

### 4. Platform-Specific Adaptations

Your messaging adapted for:
- Twitter bio
- Tweet
- Landing page hero
- Product Hunt description

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be specific** - "Indie makers" is better than "people who build things"
2. **Use real user pain points** - Not what you assume, but what they actually say
3. **Pick one user** - Not "startups, enterprises, freelancers, and students"
4. **Say what you're NOT** - This helps clarify your positioning
5. **Choose the one-liner that makes you least uncomfortable** - That's usually the right one

---

## Common Mistakes

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Feature soup ("We have AI, analytics, collaboration, integrations...")
- ❌ Jargon overload ("Leverage robust synergy to optimize...")
- ❌ Being for everyone
- ❌ Burying the lede (starting with "Founded in 2023...")
- ❌ No differentiation ("The best X for Y")
