## 🚀 Launch Platforms

Where to launch your product and get your first users.

### Product Hunt
**What:** Daily leaderboard of new products
**Best for:** Consumer apps, dev tools, SaaS
**Tips:** Launch Tue-Thu, engage with every comment, don't beg for upvotes
**Learn more:** [Chapter 07 - Launch Day](../playbook/07-launch-day.md)

### Indie Hackers
**What:** Community of indie founders
**Best for:** Bootstrapped products, side projects, revenue-focused products
**Tips:** Share revenue numbers, be authentic, engage with others first
**Sections:** "Show IH", "Ask IH", "Share Your Product"

### Hacker News
**What:** Tech news and community
**Best for:** Developer tools, open-source projects, technical products
**Tips:** Post "Show HN: [Product] — [one-liner]", be technical not salesy, expect tough feedback
**Learn more:** [Chapter 07 - Launch Day](../playbook/07-launch-day.md)

### Reddit
**What:** Community forums on every topic
**Best for:** Niche products with specific audiences
**Tips:** Read subreddit rules first, be helpful not promotional, engage with comments
**Relevant subreddits:** r/SideProject, r/IndieDevelopers, r/SaaS, r/Entrepreneur
**Learn more:** [Chapter 08 - First Users](../playbook/08-first-users.md)

### Twitter/X
**What:** Public conversation platform
**Best for:** Building in public, sharing progress, building audience
**Tips:** Build audience before launch, share value not just product updates, engage authentically
**Learn more:** [Chapter 06 - Social Media](../playbook/06-social-media.md)
